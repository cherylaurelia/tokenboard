How it works

One number across every agent. Private by default. Built to be raced.

Reads local logs

Parses usage files already on your machine and pushes aggregate counts only — never your code, prompts, or completions.

Multi-tool

Claude Code, Codex, Gemini CLI, Copilot, Aider — collapsed into one honest number across every agentic CLI you run.

Race your friends

Spin up a community, share the invite link, and climb the board. Weekly resets, ELO swings, bragging rights.
